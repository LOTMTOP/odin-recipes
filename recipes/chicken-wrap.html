Spicy Chicken Wrap

Homepage

Recipe:

This is a high protein Spicy Chicken Wrap recipe that is healthy and easy to make, so it's good for people who are dieting or trying to hit a certain protein goal.

Ingredients:

  • 600g Chicken Breast
  • 3-4 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1 tbsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 1 tsp Salt & Pepper
  • 1 Juiced Lemon
  • Low Calorie Tortilla Wraps
  • Shredded Lettuce (Optional)
  • Chopped Red Onion & Tomato (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Slice your chicken breast into strips & add the spices listed in the ingredients section as well as your juiced lemon; be sure to mix well.
  2. Cook on medium heat for 8 minutes.
  3. Lastly, get your tortilla and put the chicken in, as well as whatever else you wish to add.
